Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you create an account (name, email address, password) and when you use FlipFinder (product descriptions, images, search queries, analysis history). We also automatically collect basic usage data such as pages visited, timestamps, and your IP address for security and analytics purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve the FlipFinder service, process your analyses, send account-related communications, and maintain the security of the platform. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. AI Processing

Product descriptions and images you submit are processed by Google Gemini AI to generate market research and arbitrage analysis. By using FlipFinder you consent to this processing. Input data is used only to generate your analysis and is not used to train AI models.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers in the United States. Analysis history is retained for up to 10 analyses per account. Account data is retained until you delete your account.

5. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us. You can update your profile information from your account settings.

7. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@flipfinder.app.
