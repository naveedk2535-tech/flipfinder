Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you create an account (name, email address, hashed password) and when you use FlipAFind (product descriptions, images, links, search queries, and analysis history). We also automatically collect basic usage data including pages visited, timestamps, IP addresses, browser user-agent strings, and referring URLs for security monitoring and internal analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to: provide and operate the FlipAFind service; process and return your analyses; send transactional account communications (e.g. password resets, email verification); detect and prevent fraud and abuse; and improve the quality of the Service. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

3. AI Processing

Product descriptions, images, and links you submit are processed by the following AI inference services to generate market research and arbitrage analysis:

  • Google Gemini (Google LLC) — used for product identification and market research
  • Groq (Groq, Inc.) — used for fast inference on analysis tasks

By using FlipAFind you consent to your submitted content being sent to these processors for the sole purpose of generating your analysis. Your input data is not used to train AI models by either provider, in accordance with their respective API terms of service. These providers may retain request data for a limited period for safety and abuse monitoring as described in their own privacy policies.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services in the operation of FlipAFind. Each is subject to its own privacy policy:

  • Stripe, Inc. — payment processing for paid subscriptions. Stripe collects and processes your payment card details directly; we do not store card numbers. See stripe.com/privacy.
  • Google Gemini — AI inference (see Section 3 above).
  • Groq — AI inference (see Section 3 above).
  • DuckDuckGo — used to search for product pricing data as part of the analysis pipeline. Queries are sent without any personally identifiable information.
  • PythonAnywhere (Anaconda, Inc.) — cloud hosting platform. Your data is stored on their servers. See pythonanywhere.com/privacy.

5. Data Storage & Retention

Your data is stored on servers hosted by PythonAnywhere (located in the United States and/or United Kingdom). Analysis history is limited to your 10 most recent analyses per account; older analyses are automatically deleted. Account data is retained until you request deletion of your account. Log data (IP addresses, page visits) is periodically cleared.

6. International Data Transfers

FlipAFind is operated from the United Kingdom and our hosting infrastructure is located in the US and/or UK. If you access the Service from the European Economic Area (EEA) or other regions with data protection laws, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in countries that may not provide the same level of data protection as your home country. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

7. Cookies

We use session cookies solely to keep you logged in to your account. We do not use third-party advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or behavioural analytics cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so will prevent you from remaining logged in.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to restrict or object to its processing. To exercise any of these rights, or to request deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at hello@zzi.ai. We will respond within 30 days. You may also update your profile information at any time from your account settings page.

9. Children's Privacy

FlipAFind is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us at hello@zzi.ai.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify affected users within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach (where technically feasible), and will report to the relevant supervisory authority as required by applicable law.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be notified to registered users by email where practicable. Continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@zzi.ai.
